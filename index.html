Ableton

We make Live, Push, and Link — unique software and hardware for music creation and performance. Our community of users creates amazing things.

Ableton was founded in 1999 and released the first version of Live in 2001. Our products are used by dedicated musicians, sound designers, and artists worldwide.

Making music isn't easy. It takes time, effort, and learning. But when you're in the flow, it's incredibly rewarding.

The driving force behind Ableton is our passion for what we make and the people we make it for.

Why Ableton - Juanpe Bolivar

We are more than 350 people from 30 different countries, based in Berlin, Los Angeles, and Tokyo.

Most of us are active musicians, producers, and DJs, coming from diverse backgrounds. What connects us is our shared belief in contributing to the future of music culture.

We believe it takes focus to create truly understanding instruments. We only work on a few products and strive to make them great.

We empower our team to explore diverse perspectives, fostering an environment where debate is valued and encouraged.

We’re passionate about what we do and improving who we are.

We create opportunities for personal and professional growth through training, development programs, and team activities.

We want our employees to love it here. We facilitate smooth transitions for exceptional talent worldwide.

Relocation assistance, comprehensive health insurance, and flexible office hours are just some of the benefits we offer.

We’re proud of our achievements, but there's much more to come. If you’d like to be a part of it, please join us.
